Topic

Pokemon

In the last 2 years the hype about pokemon increased dramatically. Pushed by youtubers like Logan Paul, a first edition Charzard can now reach up to 500.000 or more. This hype also introduced a lot of new people into Pokemon. The main goal is to provide users with a d content-based image retrieval search that finds the data to an imputed pokemon image. Goal with further development: The user should be able to take an image (with camera) from every pokemon in the real world (games, cards, merchandise). After that, the user should receive all relevant information.

Try it yourself!

You can choose a pokemon from the dataset we have and we will provide histograms and similar pokemons.
